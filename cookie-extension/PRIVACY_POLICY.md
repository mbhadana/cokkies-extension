# Privacy Policy for Floating Cookie Bubbles

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Floating Cookie Bubbles** extension ("we", "us", or "our") handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection
This extension uses the `chrome.cookies` API to:
- Read cookies from the websites you visit to display the count.
- Delete cookies upon your explicit action (clicking the trash button).

**We do NOT:**
- Transmit or save your cookies to any external server.
- Track your browsing history.
- Share your data with third parties.

## 2. Permissions
- **`cookies`**: Required for reading and clearing cookies.
- **`host_permissions` ("<all_urls>")**: Required to display the overlay on every page you visit.

## 3. Data Storage
All operations are performed locally on your device within your browser. No data leaves your machine.

## 4. Contact
For questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
